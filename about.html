About Us

We specialize in Tours in India but are always expanding and enhancing our wide range of offers, as well as adding new and exciting tours of Bhutan,China,Singapore etc. A team-based business, main office in Kolkata-India, found in 2017 and since that humble beginning it has grown into trusted resources that has helped tens of thousands of people better enjoy their travel.



What makes us Unique?


1. One travel expert throughout

Many agencies force you to deal with multiple people throughout the booking process, making it disjointed and impersonal. At Crystal Travel you will deal with the same travel expert from the moment of initial contact until you are safely back at home.

2. Visit us in person

Unlike many other agencies who do their business solely online or even from abroad, we still keep regular office hours and are delighted when with planned visits or drop-ins to our West Roxbury office.

3. Quality is our focus

We insist on prompt and pleasant customer service and the best hotels, visitor attractions and entertainment. By sharing with you the hidden places not mentioned in guidebooks, the local people you would not otherwise meet and the traditions you may not have experienced by yourself, we ensure that you get an authentic and enhanced experience you simply could not have had on your own.

4. Experience & Accuracy

Our staff are experienced, well-traveled and have years of travel knowledge among them. They draw upon their own first-hand experiences when customizing vacations for you.

5. Accredited & Licensed

Adventure Travel has full accreditation with the Airlines Reporting Corporation (for meeting and maintaining the rigorous financial, personnel, and business standards as defined in ARC's Agent Reporting Agreement) and a bond with them for sale of airline tickets.

6. Reputation

We strive to earn repeat business from you and want you to recommend us to your friends and family. Your referrals are extremely valuable to us and we know that we have to work diligently to deserve them.
